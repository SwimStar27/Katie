Katie Ledecky

Katie Ledecky is an Olympic Swimmer who has won five gold medals.

Kathleen Genevieve Ledecky is an American competitive swimmer. She has won five Olympic gold medals and 15 world championship gold medals, the most in history for a female swimmer. She is the world record holder in the women's 400-, 800-, and 1500-meter freestyle.
